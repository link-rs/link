⚠ Manual Bootloader Entry Required

Automatic reset to bootloader mode failed. This device may require manual intervention (EV15 hardware).

Please follow these steps:

  1. Set the BOOT0 pin HIGH on the MGMT chip
  2. Reset the device (power cycle or press reset button)
  3. Click "Continue" when the device is in bootloader mode
